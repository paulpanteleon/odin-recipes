Guacamole

Description

Restaurant-worthy guacamole is just a few ingredients away. You'll be surprised how easy it is to make this top-rated guacamole recipe. You'll come back to it again and again!

Guacamole is an avocado-based dip or spread that originated in Mexico. It's typically made with mashed avocado and lime juice, then seasoned with salt and cilantro. Guacamole often contains tomatoes and onions.

ingredients

  • Avocados
  • Tomato
  • Jalapeno
  • Red onion
  • Sea salt
  • Lime

Steps

  1. Cut open avocados and scoop flesh into a bowl.
  2. Dice tomatoes, onion and jalapeno and add to bowl.
  3. Add a healthy pinch of sea salt.
  4. Cut lime in half and squeeze juice from both halves into bowl.
  5. Using a fork smash all the ingredients together until you reach desired consistency.
  6. Serve with chips and Corona!
