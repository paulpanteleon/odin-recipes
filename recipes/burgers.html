Burger Night

Description

These burgers are a favorite of my family. We make this every Monday night with a side of tots and veggies.

A jazzy way to spice up the boring basic burger that will tantalize your taste buds! Sriracha is the perfect complement to these spicy beef burgers.

ingredients

  • 2 lbs. ground beef
  • 2 tbsp. Sriracha
  • 2 tbsp. Worcestershire
  • 1 egg
  • Sea salt
  • Hamburger buns

Steps

  1. In a large bowl combine ground beef, Sriracha, Worcestershire, egg and sea salt.
  2. Using your hands mix ingredients.
  3. Create patties, usually 5-6 medium size patties.
  4. Cook to desired doneness in a skillet or on the grill
  5. Enjoy with tots and your favorite dipping sauce!
