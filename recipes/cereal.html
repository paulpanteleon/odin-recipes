Cereal

Description

Cheerios Cereal is made with 100% whole grain oats. These little Os with soluble fiber can help lower cholesterol as part of a heart healthy diet* (*Three grams of soluble fiber daily from whole grain oat foods, like Cheerios cereal, in a diet low in saturated fat and cholesterol, may reduce the risk of heart disease. Cheerios provides 1.5 grams per serving.)

Dig into a bowl with milk as a delicious and wholesome start to your day. Every serving of this gluten free toasted whole grain oat cereal provides 34g of whole grain (at least 48g recommended daily), a good source of calcium and fiber, and 11 vitamins and minerals, with no artificial flavors or colors. Beyond a quick heart healthy breakfast food for adults, this whole grain cereal makes an easy, gluten free kids snack by the handful. Pack some crispy cereal in a lunch box for a yummy school treat. Or make it an ingredient in your favorite cereal bars recipe. However you enjoy Cheerios, it's a part of General Mills cereals, spreading goodness from tots to grown-ups.

Ingredients

  • Cheerios
  • Milk
  • Optional: Sugar...though not recommended

Steps

  1. Open box of Cheerios and pour 1 cup into a bowl.
  2. Add milk to the bowl.
  3. Add any toppings you like such as blueberries or bananas.
  4. Enjoy!
